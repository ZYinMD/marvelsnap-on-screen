Hit-Monkey

Hit-Monkey (known officially as Marvel's Hit-Monkey for the first season[1]) is an American adult animated television series created by Will Speck and Josh Gordon for Hulu, based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name.
 Hit-Monkey was announced and ordered at Hulu in February 2019, as part of a group of series based on Marvel characters that were intended to lead to a crossover special titled The Offenders, with it being produced by Marvel Television.
Hit-Monkey was released on Hulu on November 17, 2021.
Hit-Monkey is a wronged Japanese macaque who is mentored by the ghost of an American assassin named Bryce Fowler as he damages Tokyo's crime underworld.
[2] Hit-Monkey and Bryce then travel to New York City.
Nobi Nakanishi as Ito (season 1), Haruka's partner who has problems, but trusts Hit-Monkey.

Aylesworth also voices Yuki, a spirit tied to Japan who sees Hit-Monkey as a true warrior.
