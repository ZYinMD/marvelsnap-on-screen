From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Guardians of the Galaxy is an animated television series based on the comic book super hero team known as Guardians of the Galaxy. The series premiered on Disney XD on August 1, 2015, as part of the Marvel Universe block. As of June 9, 2019, 79 episodes of Guardians of the Galaxy have aired.
